## What Judges Will Appreciate

1. **Transparency**: Clear distinction between targets, prototype results, and validated data
2. **Scientific Foundation**: References to published research (Boreyko & Chen, WHO SODIS, etc.)
3. **Realistic Claims**: All metrics are clearly labeled as targets or prototype results
4. **Complete Documentation**: Technical specs, cost analysis, manufacturing considerations
5. **Impact Focus**: Clear SDG alignment and projected impact metrics
6. **Differentiation**: Explicitly stated what makes Hygrosphere unique
7. **Next Steps**: Clear roadmap for validation, piloting, and scaling
8. **Professional Quality**: Polished design, accessible, responsive

## Areas Clearly Labeled

- **Prototype Results**: Benchtop condensation tests, early filtration tests
- **Design Validated**: SODIS design based on WHO guidelines
- **Targets**: Daily output, treatment times, removal efficiencies
- **Projections**: Impact metrics, cost savings (clearly labeled as estimates)
- **Planned**: Field testing, manufacturing optimization

## Notes for Judges

- All performance claims are either:
  - Based on prototype testing (clearly stated)
  - Based on published research (referenced)
  - Clearly labeled as targets/projections
- Cost analysis shows both prototype costs and target retail pricing
- Validation status is transparent throughout
- Scientific references are provided for key design decisions
- The team acknowledges this is a prototype in development, not a finished product
